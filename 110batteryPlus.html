110 Talking Battery +

(paid full version without ads, analytics)

"110 Talking Battery +" is an application for Android devices (phones, tablets, Chromebooks),
that monitors battery and power parameters.
It can play sounds and voice prompts.
During charging, you don't have to approach the device and check the information on its screen.
The application will vocally announce the power type, charge level,
and critical battery health and temperature parameters.
The application presents basic information in a simplified system notification,
where you can see whether the phone is charging, whether it's overheating,
what the battery health is, and how much time is left to charge.
You can analyze many battery parameters such as:
* charge level
* type of connected charger
* battery health
* voltage (with minimum, maximum, average)
* temperature (with minimum, maximum, average)
* charging current (with minimum, maximum, average) - on some phones
* battery capacity and type
* charge/discharge time
* approximate time to full charge - on some phones
