We are trying to add more and more languages, starting from the most popular and used ones.
But remember that you can enter your own voice messages into the application,
so you can use any language with a supported "text to speech engine" (TTS).
In application go to "Speech settings", switch to "Enter custom" and on list "Locale" find your language.
Then set custom notification messages.
More details in the FAQ.
