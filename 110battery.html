"110 Talking Battery" is an application for Android devices (phones, tablets, Chromebooks),
that monitors battery and power parameters,
on changes can play sounds and inform with human voice, so the name Talking Battery.
You don't have to approach the device and check the information on its screen.
The application will vocally announce the power type, charge level,
and critical battery health and temperature parameters.
The application presents basic information in a simplified system notification,
where you can see whether the phone is charging, whether it's overheating,
what the battery health is, and how much time is left to charge.
You can analyze on application main screen many battery parameters such as:
* charge level (in percentage)
* type of connected charger (ac power, usb, wireless)
* battery health (good, overloaded, overheated)
* voltage (in volt(V) with minimum, maximum, average values)
* temperature (in degrees Celsius (C) or Fahrenheit (F) with minimum, maximum, average values)
* charging current (in milli ampere (mA) with minimum, maximum, average values)
* battery type and capacity (in milli ampere hours (mAh) i.e. lithium-ion 5000mAh)
* charge/discharge time
* approximate time to full charge
Difference between free and paid(+) version:
* free version contains ads and detailed analytics,
* paid version has no ads, simplified analytics,
has much smaller size to download, and is faster.
To support future development of application, we of course suggest paid version.
