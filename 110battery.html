"110 Talking Battery" is an application for Android devices (phone, tablet, Chromebook),
that monitors charging status, battery parameters, and power supply,
and plays sounds and voice messages about changes, hence the name Talking Battery.

While charging, you don't have to approach the device and check the information on the screen.
The app will provide voice feedback about the power type, charge level,
and critical battery health and temperature parameters.

Install the app and listen to a detailed introduction and help on how to use it.
Learn how to save power and keep your battery healthy.
Set your own voice notifications for health and charging.
You can disable unnecessary notifications, choose additional sounds,
change the message content, set the voice to male or female,
and set a fun battery face animation.

The app presents basic information in a simplified system notification,
where you can see whether the phone is charging, whether it's overheating,
the battery health, and how much time is left to charge.

In the app, you can analyze detailed information such as:
* Charge level (in percent, e.g., 80%)
* Connected charger type (AC, USB, wireless)
* Battery health (good, overloaded, overheated)
* Voltage (in volts (V) with minimum, maximum, average)
* Temperature (in degrees Celsius (C) or Fahrenheit (F) with minimum, maximum, average)
* Charging current (in milliamperes (mA) with minimum, maximum, average)
* Battery capacity and type (in milliampere-hours (mAh), e.g., lithium-ion 5000mAh)
* Charging or discharging time
* Estimated time to full charge

Differences between the free and paid(+) versions of the app:
* The free version contains ads, detailed analyses, may include test and experimental solutions,
* The paid version is ad-free, offers simplified analyses, has a smaller download size, is faster, and is more stable.
To support app development, we offer the paid version.
