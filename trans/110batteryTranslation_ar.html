tips_title_01Adjust screen timeout and brightnessضبط مهلة الشاشة وسطوعها
tips_text_01The screen is the biggest battery drain on your phone, consuming up to 70% of the power. Shorten the screen timeout duration to ensure the screen turns off quickly when not in use.
