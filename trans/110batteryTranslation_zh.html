app_tips_10_titleAvoid extreme temperatures避免极端温度
app_tips_10_detailsKeep your phone at moderate temperatures, as both extreme heat and cold can affect battery performance and reduce its lifespan. To keep your phone cool, avoid leaving it in direct sunlight or in hot cars. You can also use a case that helps to keep your phone cool.将手机保持在适中的温度，因为极热和极冷都会影响电池性能并缩短其使用寿命。为了保持手机凉爽，请避免将其放在阳光直射或炎热的汽车中。您还可以使用保护壳来帮助手机保持凉爽。
